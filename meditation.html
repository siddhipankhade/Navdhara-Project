Meditation

Meditation is a practice where you focus your mind and relax your body to achieve a state of calm and mental clarity.The goal is to clear your mind of distractions and reach a peaceful state. It can help reduce stress, improve focus, and enhance overall well-being.

Here are some basic mediatations you can try :

Note:
  1. Sit in Vajrasana (if not can sit in normal position keeping back straight.)
  2. Repeat every type for 5-6 times.
  3. You can perform it in 2-3 sessions as per your convinience.
Bhramari
  • Close your eyes.
  • Inhale deeply through nose.
  • Keeping control on breathing,slowly exhale.
  • Make a deep & steady humming sound like a bee buzz.
Kapalbhati
  • Take position.
  • Place your hands on the knees with palms facing upwards.
  • Inhale deeply.
  • While exhaling you need to pull your stomach.
  • Now relax.
Box breathing
  • Breathe in for 4 sec.
  • Hold - 4sec.
  • Breathe out in 4 sec.
  • Hold - 4sec.
NadiShodhan Pranayam
  • Left hand in chin mudra.
  • Right hand index & middle finger between your eyebrows.
  • Thumb on the right nostril.
  • Ring finger on left.
  • Breathe in right.
  • Close your right & breathe out from left side.
  • Breathe in left.
  • Close your left & breathe out from right side.
Breathe of Joy
  • Breathe in.
  • Expand your hands out.
  • Push your chest forward.
  • Breathe out.
  • Push your hands forward.
  • Chest back.
Bhastrika
  • Take position.
  • Loose fist in front of your shoulder.
  • Elbows close to the body.
  • Breathe in.
  • Breathe out.
