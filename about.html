OUR MISSION

Sakhi has been created with a single mission in mind: to support and empower women through every stage of menopause. We understand that this journey can be challenging, and our goal is to provide reliable, comprehensive, and compassionate guidance to help you navigate this significant life transition and to help people become better informed and more prepared during this time of their lives.


What We Offer

Here at Sakhi, you will find a wealth of resources, including:

  • Informative articles on menopause symptoms and management
  • Guides on lifestyle changes, diet, and exercise
  • Tips for mental and emotional well-being
  • Personal stories and experiences

OUR STORY

Founded by Esha Pansare, Sakhi was born out of a personal journey with my mother going through menopause. Recognizing the lack of accessible and trustworthy resources, I and my team Siddhi Pankhade and Samiksha Ner decided to create a platform where women can find the information and support they need.




Quality and Reliability

We are committed to providing accurate and up-to-date information. Our content is thoroughly researched and reviewed to ensure you receive trustworthy advice.
